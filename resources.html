    Primary Sources Online

    Although library resources are a great place to start research for primary sources, there are also many good freely accessible website with primary sources. These websites are either curated by University libraries or produced by archivists and recognized organizations.

    • Library of Congress - Primary Sources sets
    • Fordham University - Primary Sources
    • The Avalon Project - Documents in Law, History, and Diplomacy (Yale Law School)
    • EuroDocs: Online Sources for European History (BYU)
